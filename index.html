RESEARCH · 03

Three core problems

01 LOST

Users couldn't navigate. Too many entry points, no clear hierarchy, high cognitive load on the home screen.

↑ 40% longer time-on-screen
02 FORGOTTEN

Repeat purchasers had no way to save or recall frequent items. Zero memory aid in the entire app.

↑ 3× more taps per session
03 TRAPPED

Checkout created anxiety. Hidden bill breakdowns eroded trust at the exact moment users needed it most.

↑ 28% cart abandonment
